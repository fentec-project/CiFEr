Detailed Description

Normal cumulative sampler.

Typedef Documentation

◆ cfe_normal_cumulative

typedef struct cfe_normal_cumulative cfe_normal_cumulative

cfe_normal_cumulative samples random values from the cumulative normal (Gaussian) probability distribution, centered on 0. This sampler is the fastest, but is limited only to cases when sigma is not too big, due to the sizes of the precomputed tables.
